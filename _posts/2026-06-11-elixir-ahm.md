From **June 8–10, 2026**, I attended the **[ELIXIR All Hands Meeting (AHM) 2026](https://elixir-europe.org/events/elixir-all-hands-2026)** in Lyon, as part of the [IFB (ELIXIR-FR)](https://www.ifb-elixir.fr/) delegation.

As part of the **Tools Platform's mini-symposium**, [_"From software to science: The ELIXIR Tools Platform in action"_](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1JMs72D8yJESwqLyqerEw6RqVe8yxnJ2uPAHioJsldGQ/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.i26qccnrr613), I presented the **MiCoReCa project**, which focuses on the aggregation and curation of microbial resources, and participated in the closing panel discussion.

I also spoke at the workshop [_"Mapping cross-project outreach and user journeys for the BFSP community"_](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1z1_RaMt-TT5R9aU7MOuROzCAMfA-EH-GAWB3_LYoxgQ/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.306gbb7w1q9d), where I shared the results of the **[FAIRyMAGs](https://elixir-europe.org/how-we-work/scientific-programme/science/bfsp/fairymags) project**.

Additionally, I presented a poster on the **[Microbiology Galaxy Lab](https://doi.org/10.7490/f1000research.1120629.1)**, while **Solenne Correard** showcased the **Biodiversity Genomics Galaxy Lab**, to which I contributed.
