On **April 20–21, 2026**, I joined the **[ELIXIR Biodiversity, Food Security and Pathogens hybrid workshop](https://elixir-europe.org/events/BFSP-workshop)**. While participating online, I presented the latest updates on **FAIRyMAGs**, a **BFSP-funded project** launched in early 2025.

[FAIRyMAGs](https://elixir-europe.org/how-we-work/scientific-programme/science/bfsp/fairymags) delivers a **modular, FAIR-compliant pipeline** within the **Galaxy platform**, featuring six interconnected workflows for generating and analyzing **Metagenomics Assembled Genomes (MAGs)**. The project includes **comprehensive training materials** (tutorials, learning pathways, test datasets, and expert videos) and has been validated against benchmarks and applied to four real-world microbiome datasets. Additionally, we introduced a **machine learning framework** to predict memory requirements for metagenomic assembly, optimizing resource allocation and extending the project's impact beyond Galaxy.

You can watch my presentation **[here](https://youtu.be/nBh8A6RlTPA?si=dUrz91LUtTiNtLBf)**.

Beyond the presentation, I contributed to discussions on the **BFSP 2027–2028 work plan**, helping shape the future of this community-driven initiative. For more details, check out the **[event report on Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/19690487)**.
