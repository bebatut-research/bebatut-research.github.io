Last November, I had the privilege of participating in [BioHackathon Europe](https://biohackathon-europe.org/) 2024 in Berlin, organized by [ELIXIR](https://elixir-europe.org/). As part of a special video series highlighting the event's impact, I was interviewed by Deborah about my experiences and the projects I've been involved in across multiple BioHackathons.

During the interview, I reflected on how the BioHackathon Europe events have been instrumental in shaping my work. BioHackathon Europe has not only been a platform for developing innovative projects but also a catalyst for cross-domain and cross-regional collaborations. I shared how the experience helped sustain projects I co-lead, secure funding, and connect with collaborators across Europe. Beyond the tangible outcomes, the event has fostered strong, lasting connections within the European bioinformatics community—something I deeply value.

This interview has been used for the [BioHackathon Europe 2025 highlights video](https://youtu.be/JkpAnivMAxI?si=OxQ_EWo2EnIbrY46), showcasing the broader impact of these events. For a deeper dive into my personal journey and insights, you can watch my full interview [here](https://youtu.be/8iL3LdzjtJw?si=3hI7YSZM6a2z6sEZ).
