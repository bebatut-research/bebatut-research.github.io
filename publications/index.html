Publications

I practice open science to ensure my research is accessible, transparent, and reusable. My work is published in open-access journals to maximize reach and impact. Preprints are systematically shared on bioRxiv and BioHackrXiv to enable early access and recognize all contributors.

All publications are archived on HAL and linked to my ORCID profile. Research data are annotated and deposited in public databases, while code, workflows, and training materials are released under open licenses on GitHub or GitLab. Software versions are archived on Zenodo (with DOIs) and registered on bio.tools. Protocols are published on Protocols.io and workflows on WorkflowHub.

To ensure quality and credit, software is submitted to peer-reviewed journals like the Journal of Open Source Software (JOSS). Conference presentations and posters are openly shared, often on F1000Research with DOIs.

For a complete list of my publications, visit my HAL profile.
